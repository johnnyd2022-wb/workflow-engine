Sales Pitch

Hey x, I'm Johnny/Niko calling from Whistlebird and I'm hoping to speak to the manager or owner.

manager/owner joins the call..

Hey this is Johnny/Niko from Whistlebird - I run a Wellington Distillery called Whistlebird and we specialise in Gin.

Our Gin Whistlebird Wildflower recently won a GOLD medal at the Super Spiit Awards and a Bronze medal at the New Zealand Spirit Awards

and we are now stocked in lot of liquorland/super liquor stores nationwide

USP hook

What makes us stand out is that we use the practical application of sound in our distllation process - its how we get efficiency and a really clean finish.

We've also crafted a custom bottle and label that wraps our process into a story on the shelf

The ask..

The reason I'm calling is because we are expanding distribution in x region now.

Options to ask..

  • I'd love to chat if you're interested in hearing more
  • We've kept it simple: one case minimum, free shipping - would you be open to trying us?

Counters

Customer asks for email with info

micro-commitment options to counter..

Absolutely, I'll send you the info.

  • default - Most stores start with a single case to see how it moves, I can get that ready for you.
  • low-risk - We typically get new stores started with a single case so they can test without tying up shelf space.
  • social-proof - Usually stores kick off with a single case, it’s the easiest way to see it sell.

Which email should I send the details to?

Fallback options if above fail

  • consignment - We're also happy for you to try Whistlebird on a consignment model so there is low risk.
  • call-back - I'll send the info and we can chat about it. Is there a good time to call you back?
