CRM Updater

Keeps the pipeline records true after every email, call and demo.

What it does

The hygiene counterpart to the Sales Manager: one decides and drafts, this one records and maintains. After any sales interaction it updates the contact's stage, logs what happened in one line, sets the next follow-up date, and sweeps for anything going stale — so that when priorities are set, they're set from reality, not memory.

Use it when

After an email, call, demo or order Weekly stale-lead sweep “Log this interaction” The pipeline feels out of date

What it gives you

  • Updated pipeline recordsStage, last touch, a one-line factual note, and the next follow-up date — for every contact touched.
  • Follow-up tasksDated tasks handed to the Sales Manager to action.
  • Stale-pipeline reportEveryone past their follow-up date or untouched for 2+ weeks, so nothing silently rots.

What it deliberately stays out of

  • Deciding who to contact or what to say → Sales Manager
  • Writing outreach content → Sales Manager / Content Producer
  • Pricing and terms → founder + Finance Advisor

Good to know

  • Cardinal rule: no active contact without a next-action date.
  • Whistlebird's ~3-week reorder cycle sets reorder follow-up dates automatically.
  • Notes stay factual and short — it never editorialises or invents outcomes, and never changes a deal stage without evidence.
