# Biz-E — Brand Voice Guide

Working guide for all Biz-E copy (website, LinkedIn, sales, docs). Refine over time.

## Positioning & values

Practicality · clarity · operational control · compliance confidence · flexibility without
chaos · traceability · user ownership · low-bureaucracy implementation · **built by
someone who actually runs a regulated manufacturing business**.

Brand line: **"The flexible operating system for small manufacturers."**
Product promise: **"Build the process your business actually uses, then track every input,
output, and compliance step from source to sale."**

## Voice & tone

- Direct, practical, credible, founder-led.
- Not buzzword-heavy. Avoid vague "AI transformation" language unless specifically relevant.
- **Speak to pain first**: spreadsheets, audit stress, inventory confusion, process
  inconsistency, knowledge trapped in people's heads.
- Confident about the concrete benefit, honest about scope. No enterprise-ERP bloat-speak.

## ICP language (talk like the customer)

Small regulated manufacturers and founder/operators who are "still running the whole
operation in spreadsheets," dread audit prep, can't see current stock/WIP at a glance, and
can't easily delegate because the process lives in their head.

## Pain → message mapping

| Pain | Message |
|---|---|
| Rebuilding ops in spreadsheets | "Stop rebuilding your operation in spreadsheets." |
| Process trapped in heads | "Make your process visible." |
| Manual traceability | "Trace every input to every output." |
| Audit scramble (~20 hrs) | "Be audit-ready without the spreadsheet scramble." |
| Rigid/expensive ERPs | "Custom workflows you configure yourself — no consultants." |

## Founder-credibility angle (use, don't overuse)

> Biz-E was built from the pain of actually running a regulated manufacturing business —
> not from theory.

Keep Whistlebird as *proof of credibility*, never let it blur into the Biz-E brand.

## Objection-ready claims

- User-configurable custom processes (the process graph, not just metadata fields).
- Inventory by stage; source-to-sale traceability; one-click audit-ready reports.
- Xero integration (in progress); QBO/MYOB considered.
- NZ/AU compliance awareness. Core ~$150+GST/mo, Compliant ~$199–200+GST/mo.

Full objection handling: `skills/sales-manager/objection-handling/bize-objections.md`.

## Phrases to avoid

"Revolutionary," "AI-powered everything," "enterprise-grade" (implies bloat), "digital
transformation," and any claim of a feature that isn't shipped — say "in progress" instead.

## Example copy (starter)

**Landing hero:**
> Stop running your distillery on spreadsheets. Biz-E lets you build your real process —
> inputs, constraints, inventory by stage, and audit-ready traceability — without a
> consultant or a rigid ERP.

**LinkedIn (educational):**
> Audit prep shouldn't take 20 hours. If every input is traced to every output as work
> happens, the report is a click — not a weekend. That's the whole idea behind Biz-E.
