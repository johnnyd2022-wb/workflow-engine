# Audiences

Who we sell to and what each group needs. Skills use this to target messaging and pick
channels. Two businesses, distinct audiences — don't blur them.

---

## Whistlebird

### 1. Retail bottle stores (Liquorland, independents, specialty liquor)
- **Needs:** strong margin · proof it sells · award credibility · clear product story ·
  easy reorder · POS/shelf-talker material · confidence customers will "get" the product.
- **We provide:** retailer pipeline discipline, follow-up emails, store-specific outreach,
  sell-through summaries, sales scripts, retailer one-pagers, award/product announcements.

### 2. Distributors / wholesale partners
- **Needs:** commercial viability · consistent supply · differentiated story · good brand
  assets · reliable comms · clear pricing & trade terms.
- **We provide:** pitch decks, sell sheets, follow-up sequences, quarterly distributor
  updates, pipeline reviews.

### 3. Consumers
- **Who:** craft-spirit gin drinkers · Wellington/NZ local supporters · gift buyers ·
  premium-but-not-luxury · award-interested · cocktail drinkers · seasonal buyers
  (Christmas, weddings, events).
- **Content focus:** flavour profile · serves · occasion · local craft story · awards ·
  product photography · founder/behind-the-scenes · seasonal releases.

### 4. Hospitality (bars, restaurants, cocktail & event venues)
- **Needs:** cocktail compatibility · distinct story · trade pricing · reliability · staff
  education · simple serves.
- **We provide:** cocktail serve cards, bartender education notes, outreach emails,
  event/tasting plans.

## Biz-E

### 1. Small regulated manufacturers (distilleries, breweries, wineries, food, supplements, cosmetics)
- **Needs:** better than spreadsheets · affordable · flexible · self-configurable ·
  audit/compliance support · inventory traceability · production visibility · accounting
  integration.

### 2. Founder/operators
- **Pain:** too much knowledge in their head · manual audit prep · spreadsheet errors ·
  hard to delegate · can't see stock/WIP · compliance stress.
- **Messages:** "Stop rebuilding your operation in spreadsheets." · "Make your process
  visible." · "Trace every input to every output." · "Be audit-ready without the scramble."

### 3. Operations managers
- **Needs:** process consistency · staff accountability · stage tracking · SOP adherence ·
  stock visibility · fewer manual reconciliations.

### 4. Compliance / quality staff
- **Needs:** complete records · traceability · audit exports · evidence · controlled
  process records.

### 5. Accountants / advisors / consultants (future channel)
- Could recommend Biz-E to small manufacturers with weak operational systems, and help
  implement workflows.
