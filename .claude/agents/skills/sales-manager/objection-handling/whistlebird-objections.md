# Whistlebird — Objection Handling (trade: retail, distributor, hospitality)

Voice per `context/brand-whistlebird.md`: confident, craft-led, not pushy. Economics from
`context/offers-pricing.md`. Never invent margins, awards, or terms — confirm if unsure.

## Retail bottle stores

### "We've already got enough gins."
> Understood — this one earns its facing. It's Gold-awarded, Wellington small-batch, and
> it's held ~2 bottles/week sell-through in comparable stores. Happy to start you with a
> single case and see how it moves.

### "Will it actually sell?"
> It's in ~70 stores already and drinkers come back for it. Award credibility (Gold —
> Super Spirit Awards) plus a clear local story does the work on shelf. A shelf-talker and
> a case is a low-risk test.

### "What's the margin?"
> Strong for the category at ~$47.47+GST wholesale — let me confirm the exact trade terms
> for your account rather than quote a number that's moved. The reorder rhythm is the real
> win: ~1 case every ~3 weeks once it's moving.

### "Reordering is a hassle."
> I'll make it effortless — I track your stock rhythm and send a one-line reorder nudge
> when you're due, so you never run dry or have to chase it.

## Distributors

### "Is supply reliable?"
> Yes — production is planned around demand and I keep distributors updated quarterly. I'd
> rather commit to what I can consistently supply than over-promise.

### "How is this differentiated?"
> Distinctive, savory/juniper-forward profile, genuine small-batch Wellington provenance,
> award credentials, and a founder who knows the trade. Good brand assets and reliable comms.

## Hospitality (bars / restaurants / venues)

### "How does it work behind the bar?"
> It's built for simple, repeatable serves — I'll leave a serve card and a couple of
> bartender notes so staff can pour it confidently. Trade pricing and reliable supply as
> standard.

## Universal stall: "Leave it with me."
> Of course — can I drop off a sample and a one-pager so the team can taste it? I'll follow
> up {{date}} either way.

## Handoff
- Needs a one-pager / sell sheet / serve card / award announcement → **Marketing Director /
  Content Producer**.
- Non-standard trade terms → founder / **Finance Advisor**.
