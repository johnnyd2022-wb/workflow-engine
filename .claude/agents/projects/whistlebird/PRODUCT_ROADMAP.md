# Whistlebird — Product Roadmap

Strategic product view. Detailed, repeatable production belongs in **Biz-E**; this is the
project/strategy layer, owned by the **Distillery Strategy Advisor** skill (launch
execution → **Project Manager**; go-to-market → **Marketing Director**).

| Product | Stage | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Core gin** | shipping | 700 ml, ~44% ABV, case of 6. Gold (Super Spirit), Bronze (NZ Spirit). |
| **Solstice** | **live / in market** | Released (live for months as of Jul 2026). Juniper-forward, savory; blue+yellow label. |
| **Green Gold** | **pre-launch** | Gin liqueur: 25% ABV, 500 ml, ~$30 shelf target; kawakawa & honey. Bottles in hand; labels at printer; batch close to ready. See `ACTIVE_PROJECTS.md`. |
| **Liqueur range** | planned | Follow-on liqueurs after Green Gold. |
| **Pink gin** | R&D / experiment | 30 g rhubarb/700 ml → pink; UV → orange within a day. Stability is the open risk. |
| **Zero% gin (Dry July)** | idea | Hydrosol / vacuum / filtration / preservation. Regulatory + shelf-life + market-test components. |

## Production scale-up (structure only — no recipe)
- Current: ultra-concentrate; lab batch 1.8 L @ 20% ABV → ~1080 ml distillate → ~78 bottles.
- Interest: scale to 10 L @ 20%; consider a less-concentrated 3:1 version; more vibrant,
  sweet, aromatic expression.
- When production becomes repeatable → move execution/records to **Biz-E**.
