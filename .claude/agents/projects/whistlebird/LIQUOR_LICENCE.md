## Workstreams
1. **Off-licence with s 40 remote-seller endorsement** (Hutt City DLC) — application,
   evidence, fees, 25-working-day public notification, possible hearing. A s 40 licence
   must still be *exercised through appointed manager(s)* (Subpart 7 of Part 2), though
   the manager need not be on duty at all times (s 215).
2. **Duty manager's certificate(s)** — **LCQ already held** (US 4646 + 16705). Remaining:
   documents, application (~$316.25 default fee — confirm Hutt City bylaw amount),
   Police/Inspector review (~15 working days), inspector interview, DLC decision.
   Cert valid 12 months first issue, then 3-year renewals.

Research report: `liquor-licence-report.html` (reviewed & fact-checked 2026-07-02 —

ID, not just NZ).
