A process workflow is the ordered set of steps your team follows to transform inputs into outputs—whether that is manufacturing, packaging, quality checks, or handoffs between stations. Each step can require inventory, capture evidence, and record batch information so every run stays traceable.

You will define one step at a time: what goes in, what comes out, and what operators must document. You can add more steps later and edit the workflow anytime; changes apply to future runs only.
