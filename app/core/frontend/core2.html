What is a process?

A process is a repeatable workflow you run in your business—like a production line, a batch recipe, or a quality check. Once you create it, you can define steps (what happens), inputs and outputs (materials in and out), and run executions to get traceability, live inventory, and real-time visibility.

Examples: “Whisky Manufacturing”, “Tablet Production Line”, “Packaging Line”, “Quality Control Process”.

You’re one step away from full visibility over this workflow. Name it and we’ll take you to set up steps and materials.
